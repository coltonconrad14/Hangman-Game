Hang Jerry

A streak-powered hangman game with fast rounds, category packs, and bonus reveals.

Welcome

Hang Jerry is a quick-play word puzzle built for short sessions. Every round gives you a fresh category and a new word, while streaks unlock bonus reveals.

How to Play

  • Guess letters using the on-screen keyboard or your physical keyboard.
  • Each wrong guess adds a new piece to the hangman drawing.
  • Three correct guesses in a row charge the Jerry Treat Meter.

Scoring & Streaks

  • Win rounds to earn points and build your best streak.
  • Unused guesses add bonus points at the end of a win.
  • Power-ups can reveal letters or grey out wrong choices.

Difficulty Modes

  • Easy focuses on shorter words for quick rounds.
  • Normal balances word length with classic hangman pacing.
  • Hard raises the minimum word length for longer puzzles.

Game Guide

Use the tips below to maximize streaks, preserve guesses, and keep Jerry smiling.

Strategy Tips

  • Start with common vowels to reveal word structure early.
  • Save rare letters (Q, Z, X) until you have more context.
  • Power-ups are best used when the word has multiple blanks.

Family-Friendly Play

Words are sourced from curated, family-friendly categories. There is no user-generated content or open chat.

Ad Experience

Ads appear only within the designated banner spaces and never interrupt gameplay or cover controls.
